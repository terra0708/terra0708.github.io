About Us

Your trusted solution partner in professional legal services

Attorney Batuhan OZTURK

I graduated from Akdeniz University Faculty of Law in 2023. In the same year, I completed my legal internship in Antalya. After the internship period, I established my own office and started practicing as an independent attorney. In line with the understanding of continuous professional development, I participated in various in-service training programs, especially trainings on party representation in arbitration.

I make every effort to reflect the opportunities of up-to-date legislation follow-up, accessibility, and effective coordination provided by a young and dynamic perspective in the profession into my practice.

With our steadily growing team and legal repertoire at our office, we work to realize the ultimate purpose of the legal profession as set out in Article 2 of Attorneyship Law No. 1136.

Our Vision

This website has been prepared in line with the purpose of the legal profession as stated in Article 2 of the Attorneyship Law: to contribute to the proper establishment of legal relations, to support the resolution of legal issues and disputes within the framework of justice and equity, and to assist in the correct application of legal rules. In this scope, our core vision is to facilitate access to reliable and guiding legal information for both citizens and colleagues.

Our Mission

The purpose of advocacy is to ensure the regulation of legal relations, the resolution of all kinds of legal matters and disputes in accordance with justice and equity, and the full application of legal rules before judicial bodies at all levels, arbitrators, and public and private persons, boards, and institutions. For this purpose, the attorney allocates legal knowledge and experience to the service of justice and to the benefit of individuals. (Attorneyship Law Art. 2)
