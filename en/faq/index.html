As Ozturk Law Firm; we provide professional consultancy services with our specialized team in a wide range, primarily Labor Law, Family Law, Real Estate Law, Inheritance Law and Commercial Law.

Our pricing policy is determined transparently based on the minimum wage tariff determined by the Union of Turkish Bar Associations, according to the complexity of the case, the time to be spent and the nature of the legal process.

The duration of legal processes varies depending on the workload of the court where the case is heard, the evidence collection process and the type of case. In our first meeting, we present you a detailed report about the estimated process.

Yes, we offer online legal consultancy services via video conference over secure infrastructures, especially for our clients out of town or abroad.

Within the framework of "Confidentiality Obligation", which is the most basic rule of legal professional ethics, all information and documents belonging to our clients are protected at the highest security standards and are strictly not shared with third parties.
