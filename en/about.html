About Us

About Our Law Firm

Ozturk Law Firm is an experienced and dynamic law firm established to provide its clients with the highest quality legal service. We work to protect our clients rights in the best way by combining the knowledge and experience we have accumulated over the years with a modern understanding of law.

Our firm provides services in a wide range with a team of lawyers specialized in various legal fields. We offer comprehensive consultancy and case follow-up services to our clients in labor law, employment law, family law, real estate law and many other areas.

Each client's situation is unique, and therefore, in every case and consultancy process, we develop strategies appropriate to our client's special needs by conducting a detailed analysis. We work with our principle of transparent communication and regular information, prioritizing client satisfaction.

In the face of the complexity of legal processes, we are proud to be a team that stands by our clients, gives them confidence and strives to achieve the best results.

Our Vision

To be a leading and reliable brand in the field of law, to contribute to the realization of justice by providing our clients with the highest quality legal service. We aim to be a constantly developing and renewing law firm by blending modern legal understanding with traditional values.

To protect the rights of our clients and offer them the best legal solutions, we adopt an efficient and result-oriented service approach by using technology effectively.

Our Mission

To be a reliable and professional solution partner for our clients in their legal needs. To work to achieve the best results within the framework of ethical values, prioritizing our clients interests in every case and consultancy process.

To be a firm that makes a difference in the field of law with a service approach focused on transparent communication, regular information and client satisfaction. To contribute to social justice and always defend the principle of rule of law.

Our Values

Reliability

Gaining and maintaining the trust of our clients is our top priority.

Professionalism

We apply the highest professional standards in all our work.

Ethical Values

We strictly adhere to professional ethical rules.

Excellence

We are in pursuit of continuous development and excellence.
