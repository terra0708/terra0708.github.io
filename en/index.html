About Us

Advocacy is a public service and an independent profession.

An attorney freely represents independent defense, which is one of the founding elements of the judiciary. (Attorneyship Law Art. 1)

The purpose of advocacy is to ensure the regulation of legal relations, the resolution of all legal matters and disputes in accordance with justice and equity, and the full application of legal rules before judicial bodies at all levels, arbitrators, and public and private persons, boards, and institutions. (Attorneyship Law Art. 2) We also strive to fulfill this purpose imposed on us by law.
